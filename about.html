About

The inspiration behind me building a weather app stems from the desire to provide Nigerians with easily accessible and accurate weather information. Weather affects our daily lives, from planning outdoor activities to making travel arrangements. By creating a weather app, i am to empower individuals with real-time forecasts, severe weather alerts, and personalized features such as location-based weather updates and customizable notifications. The app serves as a convenient tool for users to stay informed about weather conditions and make informed decisions, ensuring their safety and enhancing their overall experience in various aspects of life
