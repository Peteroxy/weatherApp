Features

Current Weather Information: Display real-time weather conditions such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind direction, and visibility.

Multiple Locations: Allow users to save and track weather information for multiple locations, such as their home, workplace, or favorite travel destinations.

Widget Integration: Offer weather widgets that can be placed on the home screen, providing users with quick access to essential weather information without opening the app.
